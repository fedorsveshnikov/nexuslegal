SOC 2 Compliance

NexusLegal is committed to maintaining the highest standards of security and confidentiality for all customer data. We adhere to SOC 2 principles, ensuring our systems and processes are regularly audited for security, availability, processing integrity, confidentiality, and privacy.

GDPR Commitment

We respect your right to data privacy and comply with the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR). NexusLegal implements strict data protection measures and provides clear processes for accessing, correcting, or deleting your personal data.

For any compliance or security-related questions, please contact us at compliance@nexuslegal.com.
